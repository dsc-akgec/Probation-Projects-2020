AKGEC

DSC AKGEC is a community that fosters the spirit of learning and a student focused development program by Google developers. Students are provided opportunities where developers,designers and managers work to build solutions for community.

Powered By Google Developers

Developer Student Clubs are university based community groups for students interested in Google developer technologies. Students from all undergraduate or graduate programs with an interest in growing as a developer are welcome. By joining a DSC, students grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for local businesses and their community.

Know More

Why DSC?

The Developer Students Club provides students with the opportunity to:

  • Grow their personal and professional network.

  • Gain relevant industry experience by solving problems for local organizations with technology based solutions.

  • Collaborate with Google and lead a developer community.

  • Showcase their prototypes and solutions to their local community and industry Leaders.

  • Get access to Google Developers' resources and opportunities.

Principles of DSC

1. Connect : Meet other students on campus interested in developer technologies. All are welcome, including those with diverse backgrounds and different majors.

2. Learn : Learn about a wide range of technical topics where new skills are gained through hands-on workshops, in-person training and project building activities.

3. Grow : Apply your new learnings and connections to build great solutions for local problems. Advance your skills, career and network. Give back to your community by helping others learn as well.
